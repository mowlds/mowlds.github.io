GENERAL NOTICE: UNSAFE REJOINS

IF you spin or otherwise end up off track, you are to assume for the purposes of rejoining that ghosting is off. That means your action of rejoining may not interfere with cars on the track and if there is no space to rejoin the track, you must wait until it is safe to do so. Failure to do so WILL be punished by the stewards. If you have span on the track, you are to wait until it is safe to right the car and rejoin the race, unless 1) you are blocking the racing line. In this case, it is preferable you move away from the racing line, if it is safe to do so. 2) you would be otherwise disqualified by the game (be it for being stationary for too long or for instance for blocking the pitlane) The stewards reserve the right to review these rules during the season if required.

Ry unsafe rejoin

Stewards Verdict:

In this case, the stewards believe exception clause 2 applies and that Ry could have been under the impression that he may be disqualifed for blocking the pit exit if he remained stationary. Therefore a warning/reminder of the above rules is all that we apply and there is No further action
