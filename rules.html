THE SERIOUS RACING LEAGUE

SRL RULES AND DRIVING REGULATIONS

Race Day Schedule

Confirmation: In the event of grids potentially exceeding 20, confirmation is due by 23:59 on the Saturday or Tuesday before race day. Confirmation can be made via forums or Discord. Priority is given to regular racers.

Race Day: Lobby opens at 9 PM for practice. The main event begins at ~9:45 PM after latecomers join. Assigned cars must be selected in advance.

Driving Regulations

General Conduct

  • Fair Play: Intentional crashing or race manipulation will result in strict penalties.
  • Clean Racing: Reckless driving is penalized even if incidental.

Race Etiquette

  • Track Limits: In-game track limits are final.
  • Rejoining: Rejoin safely after leaving the track.
  • Reset to Track: Only when stuck; misuse results in penalties.
  • Blue Flags: Lapped cars must yield appropriately.
  • Yellow Flags: No overtaking under yellow unless permitted.

Qualifying

  • Blocking: Do not impede flying laps.
  • In/Out Laps: Yield to faster drivers.
  • Retiring: Pull off safely before retiring.

Overtaking & Defending

  • Racing Room: Leave space for competitors.
  • Defending: One move per straight. No weaving.
  • Dive Bombs: Only if clean and controlled.

Starts & Safety Cars

  • Starts: No intentional false starts.
  • Safety Car: No overtaking, maintain gaps, no erratic braking.
  • Do not intentionally cause safety cars.

Penalties

  • Incident Reports: Submit within 24 hours with video evidence.
  • Stewards: 3 impartial post-race reviewers. Decisions are final.
  • Penalties: Time, qualifying bans, or suspensions.

Technical Issues

  • Connection: Unstable connections may require leaving the lobby.

Setup & Assists

  • Setups: Custom setups allowed.
  • Parc Fermé: In-game rules apply.

Mass Disconnections

Within 25%: If 20% or more drivers disconnect early, the race is restarted.

After 25%: The race may be red-flagged and restarted with specific rules based on distance completed.

75–90%: Half points awarded.

90%+: Full points awarded.

If 20% of active drivers can’t rejoin, the race is rescheduled. Crossplay issues may force a PS5/4-only restart.

Point Scoring

25–18–15–12–10–8–6–4–2–1 system. No fastest lap point. Ties resolved by wins, then poles, then fastest laps.

Game Settings

Session Options

  • Players: 20
  • Practice: None
  • Qualifying: Short
  • Race Distance: 50%
  • Weather: Dynamic

Race Settings

  • Performance: Equal
  • Damage: Reduced
  • SC: Reduced | Red Flags: Increased
  • Corner Cutting: Strict
  • Manual Starts, Immersive Pit & SC

Assists

  • Steering/Braking Assist: Off
  • ABS/TC: Personal Preference
  • Gearbox, Racing Line: Personal Preference
  • Pit Assist: On

Note: Ask for clarification if anything is unclear. Drivers are responsible for understanding the rules. Ignorance is not an excuse.
